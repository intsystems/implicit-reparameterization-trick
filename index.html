Implicit Reparameterization Trick

A PyTorch library implementing implicit reparameterization gradients for continuous distributions that lack tractable inverse CDFs.

Based on: M. Figurnov, S. Mohamed, A. Mnih, Implicit Reparameterization Gradients, NeurIPS 2018.
