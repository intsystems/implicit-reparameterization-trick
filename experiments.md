# Experiments

## VAE on MNIST

Following the experimental setup from Table 4 of the paper:

- Dataset: dynamically binarized MNIST (60K train, 10K test)
- Architecture: FC encoder (784-256-128) and decoder (128-256-784)
- Optimizer: Adam, lr=1e-3
- KL annealing: linear warmup over 30K steps
- Latent dimensions: D = 2, 10, 20
- Distributions: Normal, Gamma(0.3, 0.3), Beta(uniform), VonMises(uniform)

Results and visualizations are generated by `code/generate_figures.py`
and displayed in the [README](README.md).
